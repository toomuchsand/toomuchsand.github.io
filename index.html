Welcome to the Calder Valley Railway Ale Trail Webpage

An alternative to the Transpennine Ale Trail on the Halifax to Manchester branch of the Calder Valley Railway Line.

Background

This page will eventually be a full guide to the pubs serving real ale that can be found on the Western end of the Calder Valley (or Caldervale) Railway Line, on the leg from Halifax to Manchester Victoria.

There are some trains from Halifax that don't stop at Sowerby Bridge or Mytholmroyd, but once you get past about 12 O'clock there are trains approximately every half hour.

A list of all the stations on the route follows (on this first outing we started at Sowerby Bridge and travelled towards Manchester, picking a few stops that looked interesting to start with. You can choose your own stops, or you can do this in the opposite direction its up to you!)...

  • Halifax
  • Sowerby Bridge
  • Mytholmroyd
  • Hebden Bridge
  • Todmorden
  • Walsden
  • Littleborough
  • Smithy Bridge
  • Rochdale
  • Castleton
  • Mills Hill
  • Moston
  • Manchester Victoria
